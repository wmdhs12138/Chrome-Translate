# 小G翻译 Privacy Policy

小G翻译 sends selected page text to Google Translate only after the user clicks the floating translate icon.

The extension does not operate its own server and does not sell or share data with the developer. Translation and text-to-speech requests are sent directly to Google Translate endpoints:

- `https://translate.googleapis.com/translate_a/single`
The extension does not store preferences, selected text, or translation history.

No analytics, advertising, tracking, or account system is included.
